# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-14

This page describes how information is handled when using the OpenAI GPT Assistant for the Google Drive Permission Manager project.

## Summary
- The GPT does not directly access your Google Workspace or other systems.
- Any data you paste into the chat is sent to OpenAI and may be logged per OpenAI's policies.
- The project may host a read-only backend that serves documentation files; it does not collect personal data.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data directly. If you share information in the chat, it is processed by OpenAI to generate responses.

## Data Use
Information shared in the chat is used only to provide setup guidance and troubleshooting help.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties. OpenAI may process and store chat data according to their policies.

## Data Retention
We do not store chat data. Retention is governed by OpenAI.
